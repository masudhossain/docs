## Comment Features

- **Threaded Conversations** - Reply to specific comments for organized discussions
- **@Mentions** - Tag team members to get their attention on important items
- **File Attachments** - Share screenshots, documents, or reference materials
- **Private Comments** - Internal team discussions hidden from clients
- **Real-time Notifications** - Get instant alerts when someone responds
- **Comment History** - Full searchable record of all task discussions

## Best Practices

1. **Be Specific** - Reference exact details, requirements, or issues
2. **Use @Mentions** - Tag relevant team members for faster responses
3. **Include Context** - Provide background information for clarity
4. **Attach Files** - Share visual examples, mockups, or reference materials
5. **Mark Resolved** - Close comment threads when issues are addressed
6. **Keep It Professional** - Maintain professional tone, especially with client access

## Communication Guidelines

- **Task Updates** - Use comments to provide progress updates and status changes
- **Question Resolution** - Ask and answer questions directly on relevant tasks
- **Feedback Exchange** - Give and receive feedback on work in progress
- **Problem Solving** - Collaborate on solutions to technical or creative challenges
- **Decision Documentation** - Record important decisions and their reasoning

<Tip>
  Use private comments for internal discussions about scope, pricing, or client concerns that shouldn't be visible to clients.
</Tip>
