## Available Reports

- **Individual Time Reports** - Track each team member's time allocation and productivity
- **Project Time Analysis** - See total time spent on projects vs. estimates
- **Billable vs Non-Billable** - Understand the ratio of billable to internal time
- **Client Time Breakdown** - Analyze time distribution across different clients
- **Task Efficiency** - Compare estimated vs. actual time for similar tasks
- **Overtime Tracking** - Monitor team workload and identify burnout risks

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Utilization Rate** - Percentage of time spent on billable work
2. **Project Profitability** - Revenue vs. time investment analysis
3. **Team Productivity** - Output per hour across different team members
4. **Estimation Accuracy** - How well you predict task and project duration
5. **Peak Performance Hours** - When your team is most productive
6. **Client Profitability** - Which clients provide the best time-to-revenue ratio

## Report Customization

- **Date Ranges** - Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or custom periods
- **Team Filters** - Focus on specific team members or departments
- **Project Categories** - Group by project type, client, or service offering
- **Export Options** - PDF, CSV, or Excel formats for external sharing
- **Automated Delivery** - Schedule regular report delivery to stakeholders

## Using Reports for Business Decisions

1. **Resource Planning** - Allocate team members based on availability and skills
2. **Pricing Strategy** - Set rates based on actual time investment data
3. **Process Improvement** - Identify bottlenecks and inefficient workflows
4. **Team Development** - Provide feedback and training based on performance data
5. **Client Management** - Discuss scope and timeline adjustments with data backing
