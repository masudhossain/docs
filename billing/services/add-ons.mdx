## Service Add-ons

Add-ons allow you to extend your core services with additional features or capabilities that clients can purchase alongside your main service packages.

## Key Features

- Create unlimited add-on services
- Set custom pricing for each add-on
- Bundle add-ons with main services
- Track add-on performance and revenue

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Services** in your dashboard
2. Click **Create Add-on**
3. Configure your add-on settings
4. Set pricing and availability
5. Launch your add-on to clients
