## Credit Management

- **Issue Credits** - Provide credits for service issues or cancellations
- **Track Balances** - Monitor client credit balances in real-time
- **Auto-Apply** - Automatically apply credits to future invoices
- **Credit History** - View complete credit transaction history

## Common Use Cases

1. **Service Refunds** - Issue credits for unsatisfactory work
2. **Promotional Credits** - Reward loyal clients with account credits
3. **Prepaid Services** - Allow clients to prepay and use credits
4. **Billing Adjustments** - Correct billing errors with credit adjustments
