## Discount Types

- **Percentage Discounts** - Offer a percentage off the service price
- **Fixed Amount** - Provide a specific dollar amount discount
- **First Month Free** - Give new subscribers their first month at no cost
- **Limited Time Offers** - Create urgency with time-sensitive deals

## Managing Discounts

1. Go to **Services > Discounts**
2. Click **Create New Discount**
3. Set discount parameters and limitations
4. Share discount codes with clients
5. Monitor usage and performance
