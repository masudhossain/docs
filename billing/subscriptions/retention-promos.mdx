## Retention Strategies

- **Discount Offers** - Temporary price reductions to prevent cancellation
- **Free Months** - Offer complimentary service periods
- **Plan Downgrades** - Suggest lower-cost alternatives
- **Pause Options** - Allow temporary subscription pausing
- **Personal Outreach** - Direct communication with at-risk clients

## Implementation

1. **Identify At-Risk Clients** - Track cancellation indicators
2. **Create Targeted Offers** - Design specific retention promotions
3. **Automated Triggers** - Set up automatic promo delivery
4. **Track Success Rates** - Monitor retention campaign effectiveness
5. **Optimize Offers** - Refine promotions based on performance data
