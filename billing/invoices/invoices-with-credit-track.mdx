## Credit Integration Features

- **Automatic Application** - Credits are automatically applied to new invoices
- **Partial Credit Usage** - Apply credits partially if they exceed invoice amount
- **Credit Balance Display** - Show remaining credit balances on invoices
- **Manual Adjustments** - Override automatic credit application when needed
- **Credit History** - Track all credit applications and adjustments

## Invoice Process

1. **Generate Invoice** - Create invoice for services or time tracked
2. **Check Credits** - System automatically checks for available credits
3. **Apply Credits** - Credits are applied to reduce invoice total
4. **Update Balance** - Client credit balance is updated accordingly
5. **Send Invoice** - Final invoice reflects credit applications and remaining balance
