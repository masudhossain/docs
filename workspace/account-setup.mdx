## Key Configuration Steps

- **Workspace Information** - Set your agency name, logo, and branding
- **Time Zone & Locale** - Configure regional settings for accurate scheduling
- **Notification Preferences** - Control how and when you receive updates
- **Security Settings** - Enable two-factor authentication and access controls
- **Integration Setup** - Connect your essential tools and services

## Getting Started

1. **Complete Profile** - Add your agency details and contact information
2. **Upload Branding** - Add your logo and customize workspace appearance
3. **Set Preferences** - Configure timezone, language, and notification settings
4. **Security Configuration** - Enable 2FA and set password policies
5. **Initial Integrations** - Connect email, calendar, and key business tools

<Tip>
  Take time to properly configure these settings initially - it will save hours of adjustment later as your team grows.
</Tip>
